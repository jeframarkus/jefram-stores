Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?
Simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll receive order confirmation via WhatsApp.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Cash on Delivery and Mobile Money (MTN and Airtel). For mobile money, please send payment and upload your transaction screenshot during checkout.
How long does delivery take?
Delivery typically takes 2-3 business days within Kampala. Other locations may take longer. You'll receive a tracking number once your order is confirmed.
How do I track my order?
Go to your profile and click on "My Orders". Each order has a unique tracking number and status. You can also contact us via WhatsApp for updates.
Can I cancel my order?
Yes, you can cancel orders that are still pending or pending payment. Simply go to "My Orders" and click "Cancel Order".
