## 💡 Pro Tips

### Timing
- **Best**: Friday-Sunday evenings (5-10 PM)
- **Duration**: 2-6 hours for urgency, 24h for volume
- **Frequency**: 2-3 times per week for engagement

### Pricing
- **Quick Flash** (2h): 30-50% discount, 10-20 items
- **Daily Deal** (6-12h): 20-35% discount, 50-100 items
- **Weekend** (24-48h): 15-25% discount, 100-300 items

### Strategy
- Undercount inventory by 20% (creates urgency)
- Use popular products (fast moving)
- Mix low and high discount tiers
- Monitor depletion rate
- Adjust based on performance
